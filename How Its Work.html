How It Works

A quick overview of how incluziv cloud retail works

Welcome Customers

A 360 degree CRM helps you capture each footfall at your store. As soon as customer arrives, capture all interactions, namely the products they shortlist, the quotation prepared. Plan visits to the customers house for measurements. Capture the measurements and keep them forever The revision in quotations. Whatever it takes for you to finalize an order becomes a part of your application.

Collect payments

Collect payments in part or full against an order or just from a customer who has walked- in. Keep track of all payments received from customers and all payments made to vendors

Make Purchases

Ensure that you never miss upon ordering a fabric from the distributor. Each order can be traced back to the distributor and can be tracked individually. Seamlessly integrate your application with almost 20 leading distributors for placing orders and tracking dispatches.

Get order fulfilled

Getting a fabric tailored and tracking each and every order is now easy as a breeze. With total control on all measurements and fabric being issued and collected, it becomes quite a process driven method to get all your tailoring orders tracked.
